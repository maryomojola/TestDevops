Mentoring is a three-worded word
Mentoring is men-to-ring
There is no mentoring without "Men"
Neither is there mentoring without "To"
Nor will there be mentoring without "Ring"

You need men in your life journey
Men are people who will guide and lead you
People who will inspire you with their experiences
People who have been through what you are passing through
There is no mentoring without "Men"

Where are you heading with these men?
What is your goal being around them?
There must be a focus for this relationship
They are there to take you somewhere better
There is no mentoring without "To"

Like a ring that encircles everything
It must touch all aspects of your life
From the spiritual to the physical
All round inspiration for a balanced life
There is no mentoring without "Ring"
